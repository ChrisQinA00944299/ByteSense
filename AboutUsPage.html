Bytesense was formed with one goal in mind:

To provide the world a safe place in Cyberspace

As our name suggests, we are skilled in making sense of computer data. The modern-day IT systems and their infrastructure are susceptible to complex malware and other online threats. Our customized solutions can help you stay ahead of the game. Our Cyber security team is backed by years of experiences and trusted by clients from medium-size enterprises to global conglomerates.


Our staff members are backed by homegrown experience from Vancouver, Canada, as well as from Hong Kong, China. Our team not only speaks multiple languages, but we also understand the culture, psychological factors, and differences in internet laws.
